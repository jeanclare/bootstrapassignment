About Us

Fursa Sawa is a web-based application that entails an integrated Learning Management System, offering lessons ranging from basic computer use to advanced tech skills. The app enables learners to form communities, connect with mentors, and gain valuable insights into soft skills like salary negotiation and effective communication.

Additionally, Fursa Sawa includes a Salary Calculator that aggregates data from various sources, providing users with estimated remuneration for different roles, within STEM and other industries.

Core Pillars

Fursa Sawa's Core Pillars include:

Mentorship

The application will connect users with leading female industry professionals in STEM, who can offer guidance, support and inspiration. This mentorship will be crucial in helping young women navigate their careers in STEM, providing them with role models who can offer practical advice and encouragement as they embark on their journeys.

Salary

The Fursa Sawa Salary calculator will offer industry-specific salary data, giving users a clear understanding of expected remuneration for various STEM roles. This will empower young women to confidently negotiate their salaries, ensuring they are fairly compensated and helping to close the gender pay gap in STEM fields.

Community

The platform will host a community space where users can interact with like-minded women in STEM, share experiences and discuss challenges and successes. Building a supportive network will help young women stay motivated and committed to their STEM education, fostering a sense of belonging and shared purpose.

Education

Learning will take place on our in-house Learning Management System (LMS) and will provide users with access to a range of tech modules, from basic to advanced levels, starting as early as primary school. This feature will help bridge the STEM gap by making tech education accessible to girls from a young age, equipping them with essential skills that will set them apart from their peers. As they progress, they’ll gain the confidence and knowledge needed to pursue careers in STEM.
